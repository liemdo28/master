# Phase 10A Objective Engine

Status: PARTIAL, local runtime proof implemented.

The Phase 10 objective engine converts CEO objectives into strategy, projects, division tasks, approval gates, evidence hooks, metrics, and executive reporting inputs.

Supported examples include:
- Increase Raw Sushi Revenue 10%
- Improve SEO
- Fix QB Sync
- Launch Campaign
- Investigate Revenue Drop
- Create New Website
- Improve Review Rating

Truth boundary: live production actions remain approval-gated. Live revenue, QB, Toast, DoorDash, GSC, GA4, and GBP claims require fresh connector evidence before operational promotion.
