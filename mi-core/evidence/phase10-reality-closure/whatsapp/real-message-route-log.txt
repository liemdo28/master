WhatsApp messages flow through this architecture:

  [WhatsApp Cloud API] → [whatsapp-ai-gateway :3211]

                            [mi-core :4001 /api/whatsapp/mi]
                                     ↓ (routes by intent)
                    [Division/Agent routing engine]
                            ↓
                    [Response or action]

The whatsapp-ai-gateway (PID 8752) is the bridge:

- Forwards to mi-core at MI_CORE_URL=http://localhost:4001
- Routes based on detected intent:
  * "Mi" command → MiCommand handler
