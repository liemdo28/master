## Overview

Texas compliance documents cover state-level requirements for restaurants operating within Texas. Key agencies include the Texas Comptroller, Texas Workforce Commission (TWC), Texas Department of State Health Services (DSHS), Texas Alcoholic Beverage Commission (TABC), and Texas Secretary of State.

## Key Topics

- **Sales Tax**: 6.25% state rate + local options up to 2% total; food exempt; bottled water and alcoholic beverages taxable
- **Mixed Beverage Tax**: 6.7% gross receipts tax on alcohol sales
- **Minimum Wage**: $7.25/hr (follows federal)
- **Tip Credit**: $5.12/hr maximum, cash wage $2.13/hr
- **Workers Comp**: Optional (Texas is unique in allowing employers to opt out)
- **Franchise Tax**: 0.375% - 0.75% on margin
- **Overtime**: FLSA standard (1.5x over 40/week, no daily OT)
- **Sick Leave**: No state mandate
- **Meal Breaks**: Not required by state law
