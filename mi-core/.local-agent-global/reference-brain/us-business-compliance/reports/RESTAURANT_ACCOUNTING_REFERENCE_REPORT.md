## Overview

Restaurant accounting reference documents cover GAAP principles, chart of accounts, financial KPIs, month-end close procedures, and tax accounting considerations specifically for the restaurant industry.

## Key Topics

- **Chart of Accounts**: Standardized numbering system for restaurants
- **Revenue Recognition (ASC 606)**: Point of sale for retail, event completion for catering
- **Lease Accounting (ASC 842)**: ROU asset and lease liability recognition
- **Inventory (ASC 330)**: FIFO or weighted average, lower of cost or NRV
- **Property and Equipment (ASC 360)**: Capitalization thresholds, depreciation
- **Restaurant KPIs**: Food cost %, labor cost %, prime cost %, EBITDA margin
- **Month-End Close**: Comprehensive 10-day close process
- **Tax Accounting**: Section 179, bonus depreciation, meals deduction
