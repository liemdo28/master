## Overview

California compliance documents cover state-level requirements for restaurants operating in California. Key agencies include the California Department of Tax and Fee Administration (CDTFA), Employment Development Department (EDD), Department of Industrial Relations (DIR), Cal/OSHA, California Department of Public Health (CDPH), and California Alcoholic Beverage Control (ABC).

## Key Topics

- **Sales Tax**: 7.25% state rate + local up to 2.5%; prepared food is taxable
- **Minimum Wage**: $16.00/hr (2024), no tip credit
- **Overtime**: Daily (1.5x over 8 hrs) AND weekly (1.5x over 40 hrs); double time at 12+ hrs
- **Meal Breaks**: 30-min unpaid meal break before 5th hour (mandatory)
- **Sick Leave**: 1 hr per 30 hrs worked, up to 48 hrs/year
- **Payroll Tax**: SDI 1.1% (employee), UI 3.4%-6.2% (employer), ETT 0.1% (employer)
- **Workers Comp**: Mandatory for all employers
- **State Income Tax**: 1% - 13.3% progressive
