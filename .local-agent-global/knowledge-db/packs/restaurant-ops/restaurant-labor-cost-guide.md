# Restaurant Labor Cost Guide

## Industry Standards
- Labor cost should be 28-35% of revenue
- FOH (Front of House): servers, hosts, bartenders
- BOH (Back of House): cooks, dishwashers, prep

## Formula
Labor Cost % = (Total Labor / Total Revenue) × 100

## Common Issues
- Overscheduling during slow periods
- Unauthorized overtime
- High turnover increasing training costs

## Management Tips
- Use POS data to predict busy periods
- Cross-train staff
- Track daily labor vs. daily sales
- Set weekly labor budget by role
