# Food Cost & COGS Control

## Target Food Cost
- Full-service restaurants: 28-35%
- Fast casual: 25-30%
- Fine dining: 28-38%

## Formula
Food Cost % = (Beginning Inventory + Purchases - Ending Inventory) / Revenue

## Waste Control
- FIFO (First In, First Out) rotation
- Daily prep lists to avoid over-prep
- Yield testing on all proteins
- Menu engineering to reduce low-margin items

## Bakudan Ramen / Raw Sushi context
- Track protein cost carefully (salmon, tuna, wagyu)
- Portion control cards for consistency
- Supplier price comparison monthly
