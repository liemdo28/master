# Shift Management

## Schedule Building
- Post schedule 1 week in advance
- Account for split shifts, double shifts
- Track availability preferences

## Opening/Closing Checklists
- Opening: equipment check, prep completion, mise en place
- Closing: inventory count, cleaning, cash out

## POS Integration
- Hourly sales reports to adjust staffing
- Tip tracking for FOH staff
- Void/comp tracking for management review
