# Cash Flow Management

## 13-Week Cash Flow Forecast
- Project inflows: sales, collections
- Project outflows: payroll, rent, vendors
- Identify cash gaps 4-8 weeks ahead

## Restaurant-specific
- Cash business = faster cycle than B2B
- Pay vendors on terms (Net 30 where possible)
- Payroll timing (bi-weekly vs weekly)
- Tax deposits (payroll taxes, sales tax)

## Warning Signs
- Cash balance decreasing week over week
- Relying on credit line for payroll
- Vendor invoices going past due
