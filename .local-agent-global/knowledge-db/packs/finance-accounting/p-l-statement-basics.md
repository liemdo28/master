# P&L Statement (Income Statement)

## Structure
1. Revenue (Gross Sales)
2. - Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
3. = Gross Profit
4. - Operating Expenses (Labor, Rent, Utilities, Marketing)
5. = Operating Income (EBITDA)
6. - Interest, Depreciation, Taxes
7. = Net Income

## Key Ratios
- Gross Margin = Gross Profit / Revenue
- Net Margin = Net Income / Revenue
- EBITDA Margin = EBITDA / Revenue

## Restaurant Industry Benchmarks
- Gross margin: 60-70%
- EBITDA: 10-20% (good), 5-10% (average)
