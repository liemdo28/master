# Phase 11 Connectors

## Current Connectors
Dashboard, QuickBooks, review automation, browser evidence, Gmail, Google Drive, Calendar, Asana, projects, DoorDash, manual uploads.

## Contract
Every connector requires schema, normalizer, sample data, tests, audit logging, and queue eligibility for heavy syncs.

## Gap
Dashboard and QuickBooks have stronger schema/sample coverage than all other connectors. DoorDash/UberEats/Yelp/Toast need formal connector packages.
