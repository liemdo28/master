# mi-compliance-ops

Use for US compliance DB, knowledge federation, source packs, and compliance retrieval.

1. Separate source ingestion, indexing, retrieval, and answer generation.
2. Keep source provenance visible in reports and UI snippets.
3. Avoid presenting retrieved content as legal advice.
4. Verify search quality with representative queries and no-result behavior.
5. Treat source freshness and jurisdiction boundaries as part of readiness.
